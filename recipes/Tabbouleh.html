Tabbouleh Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 bunches of parsleys
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 1 bunch of green onions (you can substitute with a small yellow or red onion)
  • 3 handfuls of #1 Bulgur (very fine cracked wheat)
  • Salt, olive oil and lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Wash all vegetables.
  2. Wash the burgul.
  3. Put the burgul in a big bowl. Drizzle some olive oil on top and mix.
  4. Cut the tomatoes into very small fine cubes, add on top of the burgul and mix.
  5. Chop the parsley into very fine small pieces and add on top.
  6. Cut the green onions into very fine pieces and add to the mix.
  7. Add olive oil, salt and lemon juice to taste.
  8. Optional: Add a tiny bit of cumin.
  9. Mix very well and taste to check if you need to add lemon or salt.
  10. Add the romaine lettuce leaves all around the bowl.
  11. Serve and eat.
  12. To eat like a pro, you can add 2 spoons of tabboulh in 1 lettuce leaf and eat it like a samdwich ;)

    13. Bon Appetit!
