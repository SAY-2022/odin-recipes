Fattouch Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Romaine Lettuce
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 3 Cucumbers
  • 1 parsley
  • 1 orange, yellow or green bell pepper
  • Salt, Olive oil,Lemon juice, Dried mint
  • Pita Chips or Croutons

Steps:

  1. Wash all the vegetable
  2. Cut all vegetables into medium pieces & put in a bowl
  3. Add salt, lemon juice, olive oil and dried mint to taste
  4. Serve in a bowl and add pita chips or croutons on top

    5. Bon Appetit!
